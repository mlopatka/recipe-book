# Apricot Rub Slow-Roast Leg of Lamb
_Dutch_, _Middle Eastern_, _lamb_, _slow-cook_, _oven_  
Preparation time: 30 mins + 4–5 hours slow roasting  
Preparation: ideally 1 day ahead  
6–8 portions  

## Ingredients

**Apricot herb paste (rub):**
* Dried apricots (generous handful)
* Fresh parsley (flat-leaf)
* Olive oil
* Salt
* Juice of 3 ladles (tablespoons) lemon juice
* All blended in a food processor until a smooth paste

**The meat:**
* 1 leg of lamb (bone-in, approximately 2–2.5 kg)

## Preparation
* **Ideally prepare 1 day ahead:** Blend the dried apricots, parsley, olive oil, salt and lemon juice in a food processor until a smooth paste forms.
* Cut deep strips/slits into the lamb leg — the more the better. Fill each slit generously with the paste.
* Wrap or place the lamb in a closed container (a meat roasting bag from the supermarket works very well).
* Refrigerate overnight to let the flavours develop.
* Remove from the fridge and allow to come to room temperature before roasting.
* Roast at 110°C (230°F) for 4–5 hours. Use a meat thermometer: the lamb is done when the internal temperature reaches 60–65°C (140–149°F).
* Rest the meat before carving. Invite friends.

### Enjoy!
