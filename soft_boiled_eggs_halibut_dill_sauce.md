# Soft-Boiled Eggs with Dill Sauce and Smoked Halibut
## with Toasted Fennel Crumb
_Scandinavian_, _seafood_, _fish_, _smoked_, _elegant_  
Preparation time: 30 mins  
4 portions  

## Ingredients

**Toasted Fennel Crumb:**
* 50 g rye sourdough bread, roughly torn
* 1 tablespoon fennel seeds
* Extra-virgin olive oil, for toasting and drizzling
* Sea salt flakes

**Dill Sauce:**
* 10 g unsalted butter
* 1 shallot, very finely chopped
* 1.5 tablespoons white wine vinegar
* 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground white pepper
* 1 bay leaf
* 50 ml double cream
* 150 ml crème fraîche
* Small bunch fresh dill, finely chopped, plus extra to serve

**Eggs & Fish:**
* 4 medium or large free-range eggs (at room temperature)
* 100 g smoked halibut (charcuterie-style sliced)
* Lemon wedges, to serve

## Preparation
1. **Make the fennel crumb:** Put the rye sourdough in a food processor and pulse until you have a mix of fine and medium breadcrumbs (or grate on the coarse side of a box grater). Heat a small frying pan over medium heat, toast the fennel seeds until fragrant, then add the breadcrumbs with a splash of olive oil. Toast for a further 5–6 minutes until golden. Sprinkle with sea salt flakes and set aside.
2. **Make the dill sauce:** Heat butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Fry the shallot for 6–7 minutes until softened. Add white wine vinegar, white pepper and bay leaf; simmer for 1 minute until most of the liquid has evaporated. Reduce heat, discard the bay leaf, then pour in the double cream and crème fraîche. Season with salt and pepper. Stir in the fresh dill.
3. **Boil the eggs:** Bring a large pan of water to the boil. Lower in the eggs and cook for 6.5–7 minutes (depending on how runny you like them). Remove and run briefly under cold water until cool enough to handle. Carefully peel.
4. **Assemble:** Divide the dill sauce equally among 4 plates. Top with slices of smoked halibut. Halve the eggs and place 2 halves on top of the fish on each plate. Scatter over the fennel breadcrumbs, drizzle with olive oil and serve with a lemon wedge.

### Enjoy!
