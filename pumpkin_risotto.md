# Pumpkin Risotto
## Pompoenrisotto
_Italian_, _vegetarian_, _risotto_, _stovetop_, _autumn_  
Preparation time: 20 mins + 30–35 mins cooking  
6–8 portions  

## Ingredients
* 1 pumpkin (800 g – 1 kg), peeled, seeded and cubed
* 2 tablespoons olive oil
* Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
* 1.4 L (6 cups) vegetable or chicken bouillon (stock), heated
* 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
* 1/2 cup onions, finely minced
* 360 g (1.5 cups) Arborio rice
* 125 ml (1/2 cup) dry white wine
* 1 teaspoon saffron threads
* 1 cup button mushrooms, sliced and fried separately
* 1 cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated

## Preparation
* Toss the pumpkin cubes in olive oil, salt and pepper. Roast at 200°C (400°F) for 20–25 minutes until golden and tender. Mash or purée half of the roasted pumpkin; keep the other half as whole cubes.
* In a small covered saucepan, keep the bouillon warm over low heat.
* In a heavy-bottomed pot or Dutch oven, melt the butter over medium heat. Sauté the onions for about 10 minutes until soft and translucent.
* Add the Arborio rice and stir to coat every grain with butter.
* Add the wine and cook for 2 minutes, stirring, until absorbed.
* Add 2 cups of warm bouillon to the rice, along with the saffron and 1/2 teaspoon pepper. Stir and simmer until the stock is absorbed, 5–10 minutes.
* Stir in the fried mushrooms and the mashed/puréed half of the pumpkin.
* Continue adding stock 2 ladlefuls at a time, stirring frequently. Each time, cook until the mixture looks slightly dry before adding more. This process takes about 16–18 minutes total.
* When the rice is creamy and al dente, remove from heat. Stir in the remaining roasted pumpkin cubes and the Parmesan. Taste and adjust seasoning.
* Serve immediately.
