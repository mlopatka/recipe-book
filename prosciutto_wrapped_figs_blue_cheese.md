# Prosciutto-Wrapped Figs with Blue Cheese & Balsamic Glaze
_Italian_, _appetizer_, _oven_, _finger-food_  
Preparation time: 10 mins + 15 mins baking  
6 portions (1 fig each)  

## Ingredients
* 6 figs
* 6 slices prosciutto
* 150 g (5 oz) soft blue cheese (such as Gorgonzola), chopped

**Balsamic glaze:**
* 2 tablespoons butter
* 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
* 1 teaspoon brown sugar

## Preparation
1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
2. Cut a cross on the top of each fig and gently press at the base to open slightly.
3. Wrap a slice of prosciutto around each fig. Place a little blue cheese in the opening.
4. **Make the glaze:** Melt butter in a small ovenproof frying pan over medium heat. Remove from heat. Stir in balsamic vinegar and brown sugar.
5. Place figs upright in the pan so they fit snugly.
6. Bake for 15 minutes until the cheese has melted and the figs have softened slightly.
7. Serve immediately.

### Enjoy!
