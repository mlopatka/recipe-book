# Pierogi Dough
_Polish_, _pasta_, _vegetarian_  
Preparation time: ⚠️ TODO — not recorded in source  

## Ingredients

**Full batch (makes ~96 pierogi):**
* 8 cups all-purpose flour
* 8 large eggs, beaten, at room temperature
* 2 teaspoons salt
* 1.3 cups lukewarm water

**Small batch (makes ~24 pierogi):**
* 2.5 cups all-purpose flour
* 2.5 large eggs, beaten, at room temperature
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 0.4 cup lukewarm water

## Preparation
⚠️ **Full preparation instructions were not included in the source file.** Standard pierogi dough method:

* Combine flour and salt in a large bowl. Make a well in the centre.
* Add beaten eggs and most of the lukewarm water. Mix together until a dough forms, adding more water as needed to bring the dough together.
* Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for 5–8 minutes until smooth and elastic. Wrap in cling film and rest for 30 minutes.
* Roll dough out thin (about 2–3 mm). Cut into circles using a glass or round cutter (~8 cm diameter). Fill, fold and crimp edges to seal.
* Boil in salted water until pierogi float to the surface, then cook for a further 2–3 minutes. Optionally pan-fry in butter after boiling for a crispy finish.
