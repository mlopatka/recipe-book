# Thanksgiving Pumpkin Soup
_American_, _Dutch_, _soup_, _vegetarian_, _autumn_, _festive_  
Preparation time: 20 mins + 45–60 mins cooking  
6–8 portions  

## Ingredients
* 1 kg pumpkin, chopped
* 6 potatoes, chopped
* Water, enough to cover the vegetables completely
* 2 cubes vegetable stock
* Salt and pepper, to taste
* 125–200 ml red curry paste
* 400 ml coconut milk
* Masala curry powder, to taste
* 250 ml yoghurt

## Preparation
1. Place the chopped pumpkin and potatoes in a large pot. Cover completely with water.
2. Boil for 45–60 minutes until very soft.
3. Purée the softened vegetables with a hand blender until completely smooth.
4. While the purée is hot, stir in the vegetable stock cubes, salt and pepper, red curry paste, coconut milk and masala curry powder.
5. Stir in the yoghurt to add creaminess and balance the heat.
6. Add more water if the soup is too thick.
7. Taste and adjust seasoning and spice level as needed.

### Enjoy!

original recipe by Martin Lopatka
