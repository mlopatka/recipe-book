# Spinach Gomaae
## Spinach with Sesame Sauce (ほうれん草の胡麻和え)
_Japanese_, _vegetarian_, _side_, _salad_  
Preparation time: 15 mins  
4 portions  

## Ingredients

**Spinach:**
* 1 bunch spinach (about 220 g / 1/2 lb)
* Pinch of salt

**Sesame Sauce:**
* 3 tablespoons roasted white sesame seeds
* 1.5 tablespoons soy sauce
* 1 tablespoon sugar
* 1/2 teaspoon sake
* 1/2 teaspoon mirin

## Preparation
1. **Make the sesame sauce:** [Optional] Toast sesame seeds in a dry frying pan over low heat. When 2–3 seeds start to pop, remove from heat. Grind the toasted sesame seeds with a mortar and pestle, leaving some unground for texture. Add soy sauce, sugar, sake and mirin; mix together.
2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to the boil. Add the spinach, stem side first (stems take longer to cook), and cook for 30–45 seconds.
3. Immediately remove the spinach and soak in iced water to stop the cooking. Alternatively, drain and run under cold water until cool.
4. Squeeze out all the water from the spinach. Cut into 3–5 cm (1–2 inch) lengths and place in a medium bowl.
5. Add the sesame sauce and toss to combine.
6. Serve at room temperature or chilled. Keeps refrigerated for a few days.
