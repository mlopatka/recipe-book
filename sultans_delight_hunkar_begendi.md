# Sultan's Delight — Hünkar Beğendi
_Turkish_, _eggplant_, _vegetarian_ (base), _oven_  
Preparation time: 45–60 mins  
4–6 portions (base/side)  

## Ingredients

**Eggplant béchamel base:**
* 6 medium eggplants
* 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
* 1/3 cup salted butter
* 1.5 cups milk
* 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan (substitute for kashkaval or kasseri when unavailable)
* 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
* Olive oil, for drizzling
* Salt, to taste

## Preparation
1. Preheat the oven grill. Chop eggplants into eighths along their primary axis. Lightly drizzle with olive oil and grill in the oven until softened and slightly charred, about 20–30 minutes. Alternatively grill directly over an open flame for a smokier flavour.
2. Blend the grilled eggplant in a food processor until smooth (or mash well with a fork for a more rustic texture). Set aside.
3. In a saucepan over low heat, combine butter and milk. Bring to a gentle heat while stirring.
4. Beat in the flour little by little with a whisk until a smooth, thick béchamel forms.
5. Stir in the pureed grilled eggplant and combine with the béchamel.
6. Stir in the grated cheese while the mixture is still hot; stir until fully melted and incorporated.
7. Add lemon juice. Season with salt to taste.
8. Serve as a base/side, traditionally topped with a slow-braised lamb or beef ragù.
