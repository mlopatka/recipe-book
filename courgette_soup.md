# Courgette Soup
_Italian_, _soup_, _vegetarian_  
Preparation time: 30 mins  
4 portions  

## Ingredients
* 60 ml extra virgin olive oil
* 1 tablespoon chopped garlic
* Handful basil leaves (preferably Italian), chopped
* Sea salt and ground white pepper, to taste
* 1 kg green courgettes (zucchini), cut lengthways into quarters then into 1 cm slices
* 750 ml chicken stock (or vegetable stock for vegetarian)
* 60 ml single cream
* Handful flat-leaf parsley, chopped
* 50 g freshly grated parmesan, plus extra to serve

## Preparation
* Heat the oil in a heavy-based pan over medium heat.
* Cook the garlic, basil, salt and courgette slowly for 10 minutes, or until the courgettes are lightly browned and softened.
* Add white pepper to taste, then pour in the stock and simmer for 8 minutes, uncovered. Remove from the heat.
* Transfer three-quarters of the soup mixture into a food processor (or use a hand blender) and blend until smooth.
* Return the blended mixture to the pan and stir in the cream, parsley and parmesan. Taste and adjust seasoning.
* Serve with extra grated parmesan on top.

### Enjoy!
