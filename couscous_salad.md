# Couscous Herb Salad
_Middle Eastern_, _vegetarian_, _salad_, _no-cook_  
Preparation time: 20 mins  
4–6 portions  

## Ingredients
* 1 cup couscous
* 1 1/4 cups boiling water
* 1 cup loosely packed fresh cilantro, finely chopped
* 1 cup loosely packed fresh Italian parsley, finely chopped
* 1/2 English cucumber, cut lengthwise and very thinly sliced
* 1/2 red onion, cut in half and shaved extremely thin
* Zest and juice of 1 lemon (about 3 tablespoons juice)
* 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
* 1 tablespoon honey or agave syrup, warmed
* 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
* 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
* 3 tablespoons toasted pine nuts
* 85 g (3 oz) feta cheese (optional)
* Salt and pepper, to taste

## Preparation
* Put the couscous in a large bowl and pour boiling water over it. Cover with a lid or plate and set aside for 5 minutes. Remove the lid and fluff with a fork.
* Toss the finely chopped herbs with the couscous, along with the sliced cucumber, red onion and lemon zest.
* Whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, honey (or agave), chili powder and cumin. Pour the dressing over the couscous and toss to combine.
* Stir in the toasted pine nuts. Crumble in the feta (if using) and stir gently.
* Taste and season generously with salt and pepper.
* Serve immediately, or refrigerate until ready to serve. Leftovers keep in a covered container for up to 5 days.

### Enjoy!
