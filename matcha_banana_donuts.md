# Matcha Banana Donuts with Matcha Lemon Glaze
_Asian-inspired_, _baked_goods_, _dessert_, _gluten-free_, _vegan-optional_  
Preparation time: 10 mins prep + 10 mins baking  
Makes 7 donuts  

## Ingredients

**Matcha Banana Donuts:**
* 1 cup gluten-free flour blend
* 1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 4 tablespoons sugar
* 2 teaspoons matcha powder
* 1 egg
* 3 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
* 1/4 cup non-dairy milk of your choice
* 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
* 1/4 cup mashed banana

**Matcha Lemon Glaze:**
* 2 tablespoons non-dairy milk of your choice
* 1 cup powdered sugar
* 1/2 teaspoon matcha powder
* 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated lemon zest

## Preparation

**Donuts:**
1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
2. In a bowl, mix the dry ingredients — flour, baking powder, salt, sugar and matcha powder.
3. In a separate bowl, mix the egg, melted coconut oil, non-dairy milk, almond extract and mashed banana.
4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
5. Grease a donut pan and drop spoonfuls of batter into each mould.
6. Bake for 10 minutes. Allow to cool completely before icing.

**Glaze:**
1. In a bowl, mix non-dairy milk, powdered sugar and matcha powder until smooth.
2. Stir in the grated lemon zest.
3. Drizzle over the cooled donuts.

### Enjoy!
