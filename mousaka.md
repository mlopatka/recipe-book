# Mousaka
## Greek Moussaka
_Greek_, _Mediterranean_, _beef_, _lamb_, _oven_, _casserole_  
Preparation time: ⚠️ TODO — not recorded in source  
6–8 portions  

## Ingredients

**Main layers:**
* 4 eggplants (about 1.8 kg total)
* 450 g (1 lb) potatoes
* 680 g (1.5 lb) ground beef or lamb
* 2 large onions, finely diced
* 2 cloves garlic, minced
* 125 ml (1/2 cup) red wine
* 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
* 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
* 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
* 1 cup tomato purée (or crushed tomatoes)
* 2 tablespoons tomato paste
* 1 teaspoon sugar
* Salt and pepper, to taste

**Topping:**
* 2 cups plain breadcrumbs
* 8 egg whites, lightly beaten (reserve yolks for béchamel)
* 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

**Béchamel sauce** (not listed in source — standard moussaka requires):
* Reserved egg yolks
* Butter, flour and milk (quantities to taste/standard recipe)

## Preparation
⚠️ **Instructions were not included in the source file.** Standard Moussaka method:

* Slice eggplants and potatoes, salt the eggplant slices and let drain for 30 minutes to reduce bitterness. Pat dry.
* Brush eggplant and potato slices with olive oil and bake or grill until softened and golden.
* Brown the meat in a pan with onion and garlic. Add red wine and cook until evaporated. Add tomato purée, tomato paste, cinnamon, allspice, parsley, sugar, salt and pepper. Simmer until thick, about 20 minutes.
* Layer the baking dish: potato slices, then eggplant, then meat sauce.
* Dip eggplant slices in beaten egg whites and breadcrumbs if desired for a crunchier layer.
* Prepare a béchamel sauce using reserved egg yolks, butter, flour and milk. Pour over the top layer.
* Sprinkle with Parmesan and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 45 minutes until golden.
