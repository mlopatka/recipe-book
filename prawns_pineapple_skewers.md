# Prawn & Pineapple Skewers
_BBQ_, _grill_, _seafood_, _Mexican_  
Preparation time: 20 mins + 30 mins marinating  
4 portions  

## Ingredients

**Skewers:**
* 1 pound (450g) large raw prawns, peeled and deveined (21–15 or 16 count)
* 1 pound (450g) pineapple chunks, cut to the same thickness as the prawns

**Marinade:**
* 3 tablespoons olive oil
* 1 lime — 1/8 cup juice + zest
* 1 orange — 1/4 cup juice
* 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
* 3 cloves garlic, roughly chopped
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1/2 teaspoon cumin
* 1/2 teaspoon coriander
* 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
* 1/2 teaspoon chipotle powder (or 1/2 canned chipotle chili in adobo sauce for more heat)

**To serve:**
* Lime wedges, fresh cilantro
* Jicama mango slaw (optional)

## Preparation
* Combine all marinade ingredients in a bowl or zip-lock bag.
* Add prawns and pineapple chunks; toss to coat. Marinate for 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
* Thread alternating prawns and pineapple chunks onto skewers.
* Grill over high heat for 2–3 minutes per side until prawns are pink and cooked through and pineapple has caramelized grill marks.
* Serve garnished with lime wedges, cilantro, and optionally jicama mango slaw.

### Enjoy!
