# Roast Monkfish in Sake Broth
_Japanese-French_, _fusion_, _seafood_, _fish_, _oven_, _elegant_  
Preparation time: 40 mins active + 60 mins total  
4 portions  

## Ingredients

**Turnip Purée:**
* 450 g (1 lb) turnips, peeled and cut into 4 cm chunks
* 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, chopped
* 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
* Salt and freshly ground white pepper

**Sake Broth & Mushrooms:**
* Reserved monkfish trimmings (about 170 g / 6 oz — from trimming the fish below)
* 125 ml (1/2 cup) water
* 125 ml (1/2 cup) sake
* 2 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
* 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, sliced
* 1 scallion, thinly sliced
* 1 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon white or blond miso
* 2 teaspoons soy sauce
* 170 g (6 oz) honshimeji (beech mushrooms), trimmed — or shiitake caps, thinly sliced

**Monkfish:**
* 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
* 4 × 170 g (6 oz) monkfish loins, trimmed of outer membranes (trimmings reserved for broth)
* Salt and freshly ground white pepper
* 2 teaspoons crushed Sichuan peppercorns
* Wondra flour (or fine plain flour), for dusting

## Preparation
1. **Turnip purée:** In a saucepan, cover turnips with water and bring to a boil. Add the ginger and simmer over moderately high heat until turnips are tender, about 20 minutes. Drain, reserving 60 ml (1/4 cup) of cooking liquid and the ginger. Transfer turnips, ginger and reserved liquid to a blender. Add butter and purée until very smooth. Season with salt and white pepper. Keep warm.
2. **Sake broth:** In a saucepan, combine monkfish trimmings, water, sake, garlic, ginger and scallion. Bring to a boil. Simmer over low heat for 5 minutes. Strain through a fine sieve and return to the saucepan. Whisk in the miso and 1 teaspoon soy sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
3. **Mushrooms:** In a small skillet, combine the mushrooms with 2 tablespoons of the sake broth and the remaining 1 teaspoon of soy sauce. Cover and cook over moderate heat until tender, about 2 minutes.
4. **Roast the monkfish:** Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). In a large ovenproof skillet, heat the vegetable oil. Season the monkfish with salt, white pepper and Sichuan peppercorns; dust with flour. Add fish to the skillet and cook over high heat until golden on 3 sides, about 6 minutes total. Transfer skillet to the oven and roast for about 5 minutes, until a metal skewer inserted in the centre feels warm to the touch. Rest for 5 minutes.
5. **Assemble:** Slice the fish 8 mm (1/3 inch) thick. Reheat the sake broth and spoon into shallow bowls. Spoon the turnip purée into the bowls, top with the fish and mushrooms. Serve immediately.

### Enjoy!
