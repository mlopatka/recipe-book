# Moroccan Lamb & Pumpkin Pie
_Moroccan_, _North African_, _lamb_, _oven_, _filo_, _pie_  
Preparation time: 45 mins + 50 mins baking  
6–8 portions  

## Ingredients
* 2 teaspoons all-purpose flour
* 1 teaspoon each of ground cumin, cinnamon, paprika and ginger
* 500 g lamb, diced or minced
* 80 g butter
* 1 tablespoon olive oil
* 1 onion, thinly sliced
* 150 g quince paste (or sambal badjak for a spicier variation)
* 600 g pumpkin, cut into 2–3 cm dice
* 7 sheets filo pastry
* 2 cups water
* 2 eggs (for egg wash or binding)
* 1/2 tablespoon fresh coriander, chopped

## Preparation
1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
2. In a wok or Dutch oven, heat butter and olive oil over medium-high heat. Add onion and cook until softened.
3. Add lamb and cook until browned. Add flour, cumin, cinnamon, paprika and ginger; stir to coat the meat.
4. Add pumpkin, quince paste, water and coriander. Stir to combine. Simmer until all ingredients are soft and the mixture has thickened, about 20–25 minutes. Allow to cool slightly.
5. Brush a baking dish with melted butter. Layer the filo pastry sheets into the dish, brushing each sheet with butter and letting the edges overhang the sides.
6. Spoon in the lamb and pumpkin filling. Fold the overhanging filo over the top to create a lid. Brush the top with beaten egg.
7. Bake with foil on top for 40 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for a further 10 minutes until golden and crispy.

### Enjoy!
