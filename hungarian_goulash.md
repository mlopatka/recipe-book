# Hungarian Goulash
_Hungarian_, _Central European_, _beef_, _slow-cook_, _stew_  
Preparation time: 20 mins + 6–8 hours in slow cooker  
4–6 portions  

## Ingredients
* 2 tablespoons avocado oil
* 450 g (1 lb) stew beef, cut into chunks
* 1/2 onion, sliced
* 300–400 g (2–3 cups) fingerling potatoes, chopped
* 120 g (1 cup) baby carrots
* 10 small mushrooms, whole and de-stemmed
* 4 tomatoes, diced
* 1 red pepper, sliced
* 4 garlic cloves, minced
* 170 g (6 oz) tomato paste
* 1.4 L (6 cups) beef bone broth
* 125 ml (1/2 cup) red wine
* 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
* 1 tablespoon sweet paprika
* 4 cloves, whole
* 3 bay leaves
* 3 sprigs thyme
* 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
* 1/2 teaspoon coriander
* 1/4 teaspoon mustard powder
* 1 teaspoon sea salt
* 1 teaspoon black pepper

**To serve:** Egg noodles or gluten-free noodles of choice

## Preparation
1. In a large pan over medium-high heat, braise the beef in avocado oil with both paprikas, nutmeg, coriander, whole cloves, minced garlic, bay leaves, thyme, mustard, salt and pepper for 5–10 minutes until lightly browned.
2. Add red wine to deglaze the pan, scraping up any browned bits.
3. Transfer everything to a slow cooker. Add all remaining ingredients: potatoes, carrots, mushrooms, tomatoes, red pepper, tomato paste and beef broth.
4. Cook on low for 6–8 hours.
5. Serve over noodles.
