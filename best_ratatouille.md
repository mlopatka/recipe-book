# Best Ratatouille
_French_, _Provençal_, _vegetarian_, _oven_  
Preparation time: 30 mins + 60 mins baking  
8 portions  

## Ingredients

**Vegetables:**
* 2 eggplants
* 6 roma tomatoes
* 2 yellow squashes
* 2 zucchinis

**Sauce:**
* 2 tablespoons olive oil
* 1 onion, diced
* 4 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 red bell pepper, diced
* 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
* Salt and pepper, to taste
* 1 can (795g / 28 oz) crushed tomatoes
* 2 tablespoons fresh basil (from 8–10 leaves), chopped

**Herb Seasoning:**
* 2 tablespoons fresh basil (from 8–10 leaves), chopped
* 1 teaspoon garlic, minced
* 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
* 2 teaspoons fresh thyme
* Salt and pepper, to taste
* 4 tablespoons olive oil

## Preparation
* Preheat the oven to 190°C (375°F).
* Slice the eggplant, tomatoes, squash and zucchini into approximately 1 mm rounds and set aside.
* **Make the sauce:** Heat olive oil in a 30 cm (12-inch) oven-safe pan over medium-high heat. Sauté the onion, garlic and bell peppers until soft, about 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper, then add the crushed tomatoes. Stir until fully combined. Remove from heat and stir in the basil. Smooth the surface of the sauce with a spatula.
* Arrange the sliced vegetables in alternating patterns (e.g., eggplant, tomato, squash, zucchini) on top of the sauce, working from the outer edge to the centre. Season with salt and pepper.
* **Make the herb seasoning:** Combine basil, garlic, parsley, thyme, salt, pepper and olive oil in a small bowl. Spoon evenly over the vegetables.
* Cover the pan with foil and bake for 40 minutes. Uncover and bake for a further 20 minutes, until vegetables are softened.
* Serve hot as a main dish or side. Excellent reheated the next day: cover with foil and warm in a 180°C (350°F) oven for 15 minutes.
