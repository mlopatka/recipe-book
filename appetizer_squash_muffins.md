# Appetizer Squash Muffins
_vegetarian_, _appetizer_, _baked_goods_, _savory_  
Preparation time: 30 mins + 45 mins roasting + 20 mins baking  
Makes 24 muffins  

## Ingredients
* 2 butternut squash
* 4 small shallots
* 85 g (3 oz) hazelnuts
* 7 g (1/4 oz) fresh sage
* 5 brown eggs
* 1 tablespoon olive oil
* 1 tablespoon butter (to brush baking molds)
* Freshly ground sea salt and pepper, to taste

**To serve:**
* Sour yogurt or crème fraîche
* Maple syrup drizzle

## Preparation
1. Halve the butternut squash, remove seeds, and roast at 200°C (400°F) for 45 minutes until very soft. Scoop out the flesh and set aside.
2. In a pan, heat olive oil and butter. Fry the shallots and fresh sage until soft and fragrant.
3. Grind the hazelnuts coarsely in a food processor or with a mortar and pestle.
4. In a large bowl, combine the roasted squash flesh, beaten eggs, sautéed shallots and sage, and ground hazelnuts. Season generously with salt and pepper.
5. Brush a 24-cup muffin tin with butter. Divide the mixture evenly among the muffin cups.
6. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for approximately 20 minutes until set and lightly golden.
7. Serve garnished with a dollop of sour yogurt and a drizzle of maple syrup.

### Enjoy!
