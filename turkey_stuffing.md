# Turkey Stuffing
_American_, _Thanksgiving_, _poultry_, _oven_  
Preparation time: 45 mins  
10–12 portions (as side dish)  

## Ingredients

### Version 1 — Cornbread & Chorizo Stuffing
* 4 tablespoons olive oil
* 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
* 1 tablespoon dried thyme
* 1 bunch fresh sage leaves, finely chopped
* 8 cups cornbread, cubed
* 4 large onions, finely chopped
* 2 large eggs
* 1 box spek blokjes (smoked bacon pieces)
* 160 ml heavy cream
* 160 ml chicken stock
* 500 g chorizo sausages, halved lengthwise and cut into 1.5 cm pieces
* 6 ribs celery, chopped
* 1 cup dried apricots, halved (or substitute diced apples)
* 1 cup coarsely chopped pecan halves

### Version 2 — Classic Stuffing
* 1 bag stuffing cubes
* 450 g (1 lb) breakfast sausage meat
* 1 cup celery, diced
* 1 cup onion, diced
* 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
* 2 sticks (225 g) butter or margarine
* 3 cups hot water or low-sodium chicken broth
* 1 1/2 teaspoons poultry seasoning
* 1 teaspoon sage

## Preparation

### Version 1 — Cornbread & Chorizo Stuffing
* In a sauté pan, melt butter with a drizzle of olive oil. Add onions and cook on medium heat for 15 minutes until soft and golden.
* Put the cornbread in a large mixing bowl and scrape the sautéed onion mixture on top.
* Fry the spek/bacon in a pan and reserve the drippings (for use in turkey maple glaze). Add the cooked spek to the stuffing mixture.
* Add eggs, heavy cream and enough chicken stock to moisten the stuffing without making it soggy (start with about 120 ml / 1/2 cup). Toss well to combine. Season with salt and pepper.
* Add chorizo, celery, dried apricots and pecans. Mix thoroughly by hand.
* Use to stuff the turkey cavity or bake separately in a buttered dish at 175°C (350°F) for 30–40 minutes.

### Version 2 — Classic Stuffing
* Brown sausage meat in a pan. Add celery, onion and mushrooms; cook until softened.
* Combine with stuffing cubes in a large bowl.
* Melt butter in the hot water or broth and pour over the stuffing mixture. Add poultry seasoning and sage. Mix well.
* Use immediately to stuff the turkey, or bake separately.

### Enjoy!
