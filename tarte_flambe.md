# Tarte Flambée
## Flammkuchen
_French_, _Alsatian_, _German_, _oven_, _flatbread_, _pizza-like_  
Preparation time: 30 mins + 8–10 mins per tarte  
Makes 3 tartes (3 portions)  

## Ingredients

**Cream topping:**
* 1/2 cup crème fraîche
* 1 cup fromage blanc (or cream cheese at room temperature)
* 1/8 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
* 1 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more to taste
* Freshly ground white pepper, to taste

**Dough:**
* 1 3/4 cups flour, plus more for dusting
* 1 teaspoon baking powder
* 3 tablespoons olive oil
* 2 egg yolks
* 1/2 cup water

**Toppings:**
* 8 strips smoked bacon, finely chopped
* 1 medium white onion, thinly sliced

## Preparation
* Preheat oven to 260°C (500°F) with a pizza stone or griddle on the centre rack.
* Mix crème fraîche, fromage blanc, nutmeg, salt and white pepper in a bowl. Set aside.
* **Make the dough:** Whisk flour, baking powder and 1 teaspoon salt in a bowl; make a well in the centre. Whisk oil, egg yolks and water in another bowl, then pour into the well. Using a fork, stir until a loose dough forms. Turn out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 1 minute. Divide dough into 3 pieces.
* Working with one piece at a time, roll into a 30 cm (12-inch) circle and place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
* Spread 1/2 cup of the cheese mixture over the circle, leaving a 1 cm border around the edge. Scatter a third of the bacon and onions over the top.
* Transfer (on parchment paper) to the pizza stone. Bake until lightly browned and crispy, 8–10 minutes.
* Repeat with remaining dough pieces.
