# Pan-Seared Lemon Basil Scallops
_seafood_, _French_, _pan-fried_, _quick_  
Preparation time: 20 mins  
2–3 portions  

## Ingredients
* 12–15 scallops
* 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
* 160 ml dry white wine
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1 teaspoon pepper
* 1 lemon (zest + 2 tablespoons juice)
* 1/2 teaspoon cornstarch (as thickener)
* 2 tablespoons loosely packed fresh basil leaves, chopped

## Preparation
1. Finely grate the lemon rind, reserving 1/4 teaspoon of zest. Squeeze the lemon, reserving 2 tablespoons of juice.
2. Pat scallops completely dry with paper towels. Season lightly with salt and pepper on both sides.
3. Heat 1 tablespoon of the butter in a large frying pan over high heat until very hot.
4. Add scallops to the pan and sear on both sides, about 3–4 minutes per side, until golden brown with a good crust. Do not move them while searing.
5. Remove scallops from the pan and set aside.
6. Deglaze the pan with the white wine, scraping up any browned bits.
7. Add the fresh basil, salt, pepper, lemon juice, lemon zest and remaining tablespoon of butter to the sauce.
8. Mix the cornstarch with a small splash of water and add to the pan to thicken the sauce. Stir and simmer briefly.
9. Return scallops to the pan briefly to coat, then serve immediately over rice noodles.

### Enjoy!
