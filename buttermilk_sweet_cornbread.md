# Buttermilk Sweet Cornbread
_American_, _Southern_, _baked_goods_, _side_  
Preparation time: 15 mins + 20–23 mins baking  
8–10 portions  

## Ingredients
* 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, melted and cooled slightly
* 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
* 1 cup (170g) yellow cornmeal (or polenta)
* 1/4 cup (55g) brown sugar (or white granulated sugar)
* 1 teaspoon baking powder (yields a fluffier cornbread)
* 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 1 cup (240ml) buttermilk, shaken before measuring
* 1/4 cup (85g) honey
* 2 large eggs

**Optional — jalapeño version:**
* 6 fresh jalapeño peppers
* 1 teaspoon red wine vinegar
* 1 tablespoon olive oil

## Preparation
* Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Position a rack in the middle. Lightly grease a 23 cm (9-inch) cast iron skillet or square baking dish with butter and place it in the oven to heat up.
* In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
* Make a well in the centre and add the buttermilk, honey and eggs. Mix until combined, then add the slightly cooled melted butter. Mix until just combined — do not over-mix.
* *(Optional jalapeño step):* Slice jalapeños into rounds, remove some seeds (to taste). Sauté in a pan with olive oil. Add red wine vinegar and simmer on low until soft. Fold into the batter just before the next step.
* Carefully remove the hot pan from the oven using oven mitts. Pour the batter into the hot pan — it should sizzle.
* Bake until the top begins to brown and a toothpick inserted in the centre comes out clean, about 20–23 minutes. Allow to cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

### Enjoy!

> **No buttermilk?** Add 1 tablespoon white vinegar (or lemon juice) to 1 cup room-temperature milk. Mix and let sit for 5 minutes before using.
