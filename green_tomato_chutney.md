# Green Tomato Chutney
_British_, _condiment_, _preserving_, _vegetarian_  
Preparation time: 30–40 mins  
Makes: 3–4 preserve jars  

## Ingredients
* 1 tablespoon olive oil
* 2 large onions, chopped
* 6 garlic cloves, finely chopped
* 1 teaspoon allspice
* 2 tablespoons coarsely ground/chopped spicy chili peppers
* 500 g green tomatoes, roughly chopped
* 500 g sour apples, peeled and diced
* 150 ml cider vinegar
* 100 g light soft brown sugar
* 100 g sultanas (golden raisins)
* 1 teaspoon salt

## Preparation
* Heat olive oil in a large pot. Add onions and garlic and sauté until softened.
* Add all remaining ingredients: allspice, chili peppers, green tomatoes, apples, cider vinegar, sugar, sultanas and salt.
* Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until apples have softened, about 15–20 minutes.
* Bring mixture to a rolling boil and maintain for 5–10 minutes until the chutney thickens slightly.
* While hot, transfer chutney to sterilised preserve jars and seal the lids tightly.
* Allow to cool and store in a cool dark place. Keeps for several months sealed; refrigerate once opened.

### Enjoy!

> **Serving suggestions:** Excellent with mature cheese, cold meats, or alongside a ploughman's lunch.
