# Louisiana Gumbo
_American_, _Cajun_, _Southern_, _seafood_, _stew_  
Preparation time: ⚠️ TODO — not recorded in source  
6–8 portions  

## Ingredients
* 5 tablespoons shortening
* 6 tablespoons flour
* 1 onion, chopped
* 1 bell pepper, chopped
* 1/4 cup celery (can use less if desired)
* 1 clove garlic, chopped
* 1 whole chicken, cut up
* 1 lb andouille sausage (or link pork sausage)
* 2 teaspoons salt
* 1 teaspoon pepper
* 1 can okra (or gumbo filé powder instead, if desired)
* 1 can tomatoes
* 1 pound shrimp
* 1 package crab boil (1 cap liquid)

## Preparation
⚠️ **Instructions were not included in the source file.** Standard gumbo method:

* In a heavy pot or Dutch oven, make a roux: melt shortening over medium heat, add flour and stir continuously for 20–30 minutes until the roux is dark brown (peanut butter to dark chocolate colour).
* Add onion, bell pepper, celery and garlic to the roux; cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
* Add chicken pieces and sausage; brown lightly.
* Add tomatoes, okra (or filé), crab boil liquid, salt and pepper. Cover with water or stock.
* Simmer on low heat for 45–60 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
* Add shrimp in the final 5–10 minutes of cooking. Serve over rice.

### Enjoy!
