# Wild Mushroom Gravy
_American_, _British_, _sauce_, _condiment_, _vegetarian-optional_  
Preparation time: ⚠️ TODO — not recorded in source  
Makes: ~500 ml gravy  

## Ingredients
* 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
* Extra-virgin olive oil
* 3 shallots, minced
* 500 g assorted mushrooms (crimini, oyster, shiitake, chanterelle, or white), trimmed and sliced
* Leaves from 2 fresh thyme sprigs
* Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
* 250 ml dry red wine
* 250 ml reserved turkey drippings (or use additional stock for a vegetarian version)
* 250 ml heavy cream

## Preparation
⚠️ **Instructions were not included in the source file.** Standard mushroom gravy method:

* Heat butter and a splash of olive oil in a large sauté pan over medium-high heat. Add the minced shallots and cook until softened, about 3 minutes.
* Add the sliced mushrooms and thyme leaves. Season with salt and pepper. Cook over high heat, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms have released their moisture and are golden-brown, about 8–10 minutes.
* Add the red wine and cook, stirring, until reduced by half.
* Add the turkey drippings (or stock). Simmer until the gravy has reduced and thickened slightly.
* Stir in the heavy cream and simmer for a further 5 minutes. Taste and adjust seasoning.
* Serve alongside roast turkey or other roasted meats.

### Enjoy!
