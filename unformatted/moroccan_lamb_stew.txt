Extra-virgin olive oil
2 large yellow or white onions
4 - 5 cloves of garlic
500 - 600g lamb cut to cubes 3cm (bone in or boneless) trimmed and sliced
2 red paprikas
1 can (~300ml) green pitted olives (no filling)
1 can (~300ml) black pitted olives (no filling)
150 grams dried dates (pitted)
150 grams dried prunes (pitted)
** the volume of lamb should be approximately equal to the combined volume of the dates, prunes, and olives combined
1 small tin of tomato paste
2 tablespoons fresh coriander leaves
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 medium diced tomato

Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
Serve over brown rice or buckwheat
