Ingredients:

12-15 scallops
2 Tbsp unsalted butter
160 ml dry white wine
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 lemon
1/2 tsp corn starch (thickener)
2 Tbsp loosely packed fresh chopped basil leaves

Instructions:

1. Finely grate lemon rind, reserving 1/4 teaspoon. Squeeze lemon, reserving 2 tablespoons juice. Pat scallops dry with paper towels.
2. lightly salt and pepper Scallops on dry plate
3. Add 1 Tbsp of the butter to a large sauce of frying pan over high heat
4. Add scallops to pan and fry on both sides (~3 to 4 minutes on each side) until golden brown
5. remove scallops from pan
6. deglaze pan with white wine
7. Add fresh basil, salt, pepper, lemon juice, lemon zest, remaining butter
8. Add cornstartch with water to thicken sauce.
