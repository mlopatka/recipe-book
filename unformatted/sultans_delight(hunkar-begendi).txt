 Beispiel:
6 medium eggplants
0.3 cups all purpose flour
0.3 cups salted butter
1.5 cups milk
1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese (substituted when kashkaval or kasseri 
cheese are not available)
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

1. chop egplant into 8ths along primary axis
2. grill eggplant in oven (lightly drizzled with olive oil
3. Combine butter with milk in a saucepan over low heat
4. Beat with a whisk adding flour little by little. 
5. blend grilled eggplant and add to white sauce mixture
6. Stir in grated cheese to hot mixture from the stove, stir until dissolved.
7. salt to taste
