# Georgian Shashlik
## შაშლიკი
_Georgian_, _BBQ_, _grill_, _charcoal_, _lamb_, _pork_  
Preparation time: 30 mins + 24 hours marinating + 20 mins grilling  
6–8 portions  

## Ingredients
* 2 kg very fatty pork or lamb, cut into 2–3 cm cubes
* 30 cherry tomatoes

**Marinade:**
* 10 sprigs green onions (spring onions)
* 4 large yellow/white onions (2 blended into marinade, 2 for skewering)
* 2 tablespoons lemon juice
* 6 large garlic cloves, minced
* 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
* 2 tablespoons sweet paprika
* 4 dried bay leaves, crumbled
* 2 teaspoons salt
* 2 teaspoons black pepper
* 125 ml red or white wine
* 50 ml olive oil

**Optional:**
* 6 whole juniper berries, crushed
* 2 tablespoons pomegranate molasses

## Preparation
* Blend the green onions, half of the yellow onions, lemon juice and garlic cloves into a purée using a food processor.
* Add the remaining wet ingredients (vinegar, paprika, crumbled bay leaves, salt, pepper, wine, olive oil, and optionally juniper berries and pomegranate molasses) to the purée to form the marinade.
* Toss the cubed meat in the marinade by hand until nicely coated. Marinate in the refrigerator for 24 hours.
* Slice the remaining 2 onions into large chunks for skewering.
* Skewer the meat onto metal spikes, placing an onion chunk or cherry tomato on each end and inserting a tomato or onion between every 2 pieces of meat.
* Grill over charcoal (strongly preferred) or very high-heat gas, rotating the skewers frequently, until the meat is charred on the outside and cooked through.
