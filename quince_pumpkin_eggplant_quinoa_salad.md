# Quince, Pumpkin, Eggplant & Quinoa Hot Salad
_Middle Eastern_, _vegetarian_, _salad_, _oven_  
Preparation time: 30 mins + 60 mins roasting  
4–6 portions  

## Ingredients
* 1/4 cup olive oil, plus extra for roasting pumpkin
* 1/4 cup uncooked quinoa
* 1 large quince
* 1 Lebanese eggplant, chopped into 1.5 cm dice
* 1/4 jap (kent) pumpkin, peeled and chopped into 2.5 cm dice
* 1 green capsicum (bell pepper), chopped
* 1 small onion, diced
* 1 tomato, diced
* 3 bay leaves
* 1 tablespoon tomato paste
* 1 teaspoon cumin powder (increase to 2 teaspoons if you enjoy the flavour)
* Pinch of salt
* Handful of fresh parsley, chopped
* Pomegranate seeds, to garnish

## Preparation
1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Cook the quinoa according to packet directions, then spread on a tray to cool; refrigerate until ready to use.
2. Wash and dry the whole quince and place on a baking tray. Bake for 1 hour, or until soft. Meanwhile, spread pumpkin on a separate baking tray, drizzle with olive oil and toss to coat. Bake for 20–30 minutes until tender (it should hold its shape).
3. Heat the 1/4 cup of olive oil in a large frying pan over high heat. Cook the diced eggplant until golden and tender. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
4. Add the onion to the pan and sauté for 5 minutes until soft. Add capsicum, bay leaves and cumin; reduce heat to low-medium, cover and cook for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
5. Peel and dice the roasted quince into 2.5 cm pieces.
6. Add tomato and tomato paste to the capsicum mixture and cook for a few minutes. Add the roasted pumpkin, quince, eggplant, cooked quinoa and parsley. Gently mix together.
7. Season to taste. Transfer to a large bowl, garnish with pomegranate seeds, and serve warm.

### Enjoy!
