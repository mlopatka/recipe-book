# Kotlety Rybne z Łososia
## Salmon Fishcakes with Young Cabbage Slaw
_Polish_, _seafood_, _fish_, _pan-fried_  
Preparation time: 45 mins  
6 portions (382 kcal each)  

## Ingredients

**Salmon fishcakes (kotlety rybne):**
* 600 g salmon
* 200 g white fish fillets (halibut, sole, or cod — or use all salmon)
* 1 stale bread roll, soaked in milk for ~30 minutes then squeezed dry
* 4 pieces sun-dried tomatoes in oil + 1 tablespoon of the oil
* 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped (or fresh dill)
* Salt and pepper, to taste

**Breading:**
* 1 egg, beaten
* Plain flour
* Breadcrumbs
* Butter and oil for frying

**Young cabbage slaw (surówka z młodej kapusty):**
* 1/3 head of young green cabbage, very thinly shredded
* 1 carrot, peeled and grated
* 1/4 onion, finely diced
* 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
* 1 tablespoon fresh chives, chopped

**Slaw dressing:**
* 2 teaspoons mustard
* 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar or lemon juice
* 1 teaspoon olive oil
* 1 teaspoon sugar
* 2 tablespoons thick sour cream
* 2 tablespoons mayonnaise

**To serve:** Basmati rice

## Preparation

**Fishcakes:**
1. Remove skin from the salmon. Rinse and pat dry, check for bones. Cut into several smaller pieces. Do the same with the white fish fillets.
2. Soak the bread roll in milk for about 30 minutes until fully softened, then squeeze dry.
3. In a food processor, blend the sun-dried tomatoes (with their oil) and fresh parsley with the squeezed bread roll into a paste.
4. Add the fish pieces and pulse briefly on lower speed until finely minced (not a purée — keep some texture). Season the mixture with salt and pepper.
5. Shape the mixture into 6 patties (kotlety). Coat each first in flour, then in beaten egg, then in breadcrumbs. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight) to firm up.
6. Heat a large frying pan with 1 tablespoon butter and 2 tablespoons olive oil. Once hot, reduce to medium heat. Fry 3 fishcakes at a time for about 4 minutes per side until golden. Be careful not to let the heat get too high. Repeat with the second batch.

**Slaw:**
1. Rinse and dry the cabbage. Shred very thinly. Peel and grate the carrot. Finely dice the onion.
2. Mix cabbage, carrot and onion in a large bowl. Add parsley and chives.
3. Whisk all dressing ingredients together and pour over the slaw. Toss to combine.

**Serve** fishcakes alongside the slaw and basmati rice.
