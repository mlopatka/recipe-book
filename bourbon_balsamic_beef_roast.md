# Bourbon Balsamic Beef Roast
_American_, _beef_, _slow-cook_, _oven_, _Sunday-roast_  
Preparation time: 30–40 mins + 3.5 hours cooking  
6–8 portions  

## Ingredients
* 1.8 kg (4 lb) bone-in beef chuck roast
* 225 g (1/2 lb) smoked bacon, cut into 1 cm cubes (if unsliced) or 2.5 cm strips (if sliced)
* 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
* 1/3 cup balsamic vinegar
* 1 can (795g / 28 oz) crushed tomatoes
* 2 tablespoons tomato paste
* 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
* 1/2 cup golden raisins
* 1 cup green olives
* 10 garlic cloves, crushed
* 1 yellow onion, chopped
* 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
* Salt and pepper, to taste

## Preparation
1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F).
2. Season the chuck roast generously with salt and pepper on all sides.
3. Heat a cast iron skillet on the stove over high heat. Add olive oil (it will shimmer almost immediately). Sear the chuck roast until browned on all sides. Set aside.
4. While the pan is still hot, reduce to medium-high. Add bacon and render for about 1 minute, stirring so it doesn't burn.
5. Add garlic, onions, olives and golden raisins to the bacon. Sauté until onions are translucent and beginning to caramelise.
6. Reduce heat to medium. Slowly add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste and Worcestershire sauce. Stir until combined.
7. Let the mixture simmer for 2 minutes, then while stirring, add the balsamic vinegar. Continue stirring and let the mixture reduce by at least one-third of its original volume.
8. Return the seared chuck roast to the pan, ensuring the balsamic tomato mixture coats the entire roast.
9. Top with parsley and cover tightly with a lid (line the lid with aluminium foil for easy cleanup). Place in the oven and roast for 3–4 hours, or until a fork pushes easily into the meat.
10. Once done, remove the roast to a large cutting board to rest. Transfer all remaining pan juices, onions, olives, garlic and raisins to a saucepan and blend with an immersion blender until smooth. This is your gravy.
11. Carve the roast and top with the gravy. Serve with roasted carrots and cabbage seasoned simply with olive oil, salt and pepper (roasted at 175°C for 30–40 minutes).

### Enjoy!

> **Suggested side:** Roasted carrots and cabbage at 175°C (350°F) for 30–40 minutes.
