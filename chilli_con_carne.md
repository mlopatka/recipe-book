# Chilli Con Carne
_Mexican_, _Tex-Mex_, _beef_, _slow-cook_  
Preparation time: 30 mins + 2–3 hours simmering  
6–8 portions  

## Ingredients
* 1 tablespoon olive oil
* 2 large onions
* 5 garlic cloves
* 150–250 g smoked bacon blocks or strips
* 500 g lean ground beef
* 1 teaspoon vinegar-based hot sauce
* 5 small hot dried chilis, minced
* 2 large dried smoky chilis, minced
* 1 teaspoon paprika
* 2 teaspoons ground cumin seed
* 1 teaspoon white pepper
* 2 × 400 g cans of chopped tomatoes
* 70 g can of tomato paste
* 6–10 carrots, coarsely chopped
* 6 fresh tomatoes, cubed
* 250 g canned black beans
* 400 g canned kidney beans
* 250 g canned corn
* 2 blocks dark chocolate (70% cacao)
* 2–3 peppers (red/green), coarsely chopped

**To serve:**
* Plain boiled long-grain rice
* Soured cream or grated young cheese

## Preparation
1. Finely chop the onions. Mince the garlic and dried chilis. Prepare all remaining vegetables.
2. Heat olive oil in a large heavy pot. Add onions and cook until softened. Add garlic and bacon; cook for 2–3 minutes.
3. Add ground beef and brown well, breaking it up as it cooks.
4. Add hot sauce, both types of dried chilis, paprika, cumin and white pepper. Stir well.
5. Add canned tomatoes and tomato paste; stir to combine.
6. Add coarsely chopped carrots, cubed fresh tomatoes, and peppers.
7. Add black beans, kidney beans and corn.
8. Break in the dark chocolate and stir until melted and incorporated.
9. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Simmer covered for 2–3 hours, stirring occasionally.
10. Serve over rice with soured cream or grated cheese.

### Enjoy!

> **Note:** The dark chocolate is essential — it adds depth and rounds out the heat. Do not skip it.
