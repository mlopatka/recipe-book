# Jackfruit Ceviche 
A fresh and easy Vegan Ceviche using jackfruit.

## Ingredients
    
	- 2 cans (400g/14oz each) young jackfruit in brine
	- 1 cup diced beefheart tomato (or other fleshy tomato)
	- 1 cup peeled, seeded and small diced japanese cucumber
	- 1/2 cup small diced sweet onion
	- 1/2 cup loosly packed chopped cilantro
	- 2 cloves garlic, minced very finely
	- 2 small Serrano chilis, seeded and sliced into rounds
	- Juice of 2-3 limes depending on salt content of jackfruit brine
	- 1 tbsp olive oil
	- 1 tsp coarse kosher salt
	- 1/2 tsp coarse ground black pepper
	- 1/2 tsp ground cumin
	- 2 ripe but firm avocados, loosely diced / or presetned on top as wedges.
  
## Instructions

- Drain canned jackfruit and discard brine.
- Add the chunks of jackfruit to lightly salted boiling water.
- Slow boil and cook for 15 minutes.
- Drain in a colander and let cool.
- Remove the tough core from each chunk of jackfruit.
- Break the chunks apart with your fingers and remove the soft seeds and seed membranes and discard
- Combine cooled jackfruit with all other ingredients *EXCEPT* the avocado
- Refridgerate for at least 3 hours for flavours to combine.
- Combine Avocado immediately before serving.

Serve with tortilla chips or in small bowls as a refreshing apertizer.
