# Bigos
## Polish Hunter's Stew

4–6 portions  

## Ingredients
* 300 g sausage (kielbasa or smoked sausage), sliced
* 2 packs zuurkool (sauerkraut)
* 120 g onion, chopped
* Hot sauce, to taste
* 140 g tomato paste (tomatenpuree)
* 600 g Chinese cabbage (pe-tsai / napa cabbage), chopped
* 500 g mushrooms, sliced

## Preparation
⚠️ **Instructions were not included in the source file.** Standard Bigos method:

* In a large heavy pot, brown the sausage slices over medium heat. Remove and set aside.
* In the same pot, sauté the onion until softened.
* Add the sauerkraut, Chinese cabbage and mushrooms. Stir to combine.
* Add tomato paste and hot sauce to taste. Stir well.
* Return the sausage to the pot. Add a splash of water if the mixture is too dry.
* Cover and simmer on low heat for at least 1–2 hours (the longer it cooks, the better the flavour develops). Traditional bigos improves greatly when reheated over several days.
* Season to taste before serving.
