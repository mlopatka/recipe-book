# Spinach & Rucola Chicken Salad
_vegetarian-optional_, _salad_, _meal-salad_, _grilled_  
Preparation time: 25 mins  
2–3 portions  

## Ingredients

**Dressing:**
* 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup
* 2 tablespoons red-wine vinegar
* 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* Freshly ground pepper, to taste

**Salad base:**
* 3 cups baby spinach
* 3 cups rucola (arugula)

**Toppings:**
* 2 mandarines, segmented
* 6 rounds of goat cheese, pan-fried in honey
* 4 beets, cooked and sliced
* 2 tablespoons pine nuts, toasted

**Chicken:**
* 2 chicken breasts
* Lemon zest
* Olive oil
* Mixed herbs (e.g., thyme, rosemary, parsley)
* Salt and pepper

## Preparation
* **Marinate the chicken:** Rub chicken breasts with lemon zest, olive oil, herbs, salt and pepper. Leave to marinate briefly while you prepare the salad.
* **Make the dressing:** Whisk together maple syrup, red-wine vinegar, olive oil, salt and pepper in a small bowl.
* **Prepare the toppings:** Pan-fry goat cheese rounds in a little honey over medium heat until golden on each side. Toast pine nuts in a dry pan until golden.
* **Grill the chicken:** Grill or pan-sear the chicken breasts over medium-high heat until cooked through (internal temperature 74°C / 165°F). Slice.
* **Assemble:** Toss the spinach and rucola with the dressing in a large bowl. Arrange on plates. Top with mandarine segments, honey-fried goat cheese, sliced beets and pine nuts. Lay sliced chicken over the top.

### Enjoy!
