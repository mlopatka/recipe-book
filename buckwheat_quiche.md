# Buckwheat-Based Quiche
## with Pumpkin, Carrot & Spinach
_vegetarian_, _French_, _oven_, _quiche_, _gluten-optional_  
Preparation time: 30 mins + 45–50 mins baking  
4–6 portions  

## Ingredients

**Custard filling:**
* 4 large eggs
* 1/2 cup heavy cream
* 1/2 cup yogurt
* 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
* 2 teaspoons fresh thyme, chopped
* 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
* 1/2 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg

**Vegetables:**
* 500 g pumpkin, finely diced
* 200 g carrots, finely diced
* 1 cup fresh spinach

**Crust base:**
* 1 cup buckwheat groats (kasha), ground
* 1 tablespoon butter, softened (for greasing and coating)

## Preparation
1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F).
2. Combine eggs, cream, yogurt, nutmeg, thyme and pepper in a bowl. Set aside — it is best (but not essential) if this mixture reaches room temperature before use.
3. Smear 1 tablespoon of softened butter over the bottom and sides of a 23 cm (9-inch) round ovenproof glass or ceramic baking dish.
4. Pour the buckwheat groats into the buttered dish. Tilt and rotate the dish to coat the sides and bottom evenly. Shake gently to distribute remaining groats over the bottom — this forms the crust.
5. In a medium skillet over medium-low heat, melt the remaining tablespoon of butter. Add spinach, diced pumpkin and carrots. Sauté, stirring, until softened, about 10 minutes. Arrange the vegetables over the buckwheat crust.
6. Crumble feta evenly over the vegetables.
7. Beat the egg mixture thoroughly with a whisk until uniform but not foamy. Carefully pour over the vegetables and feta.
8. Bake until the centre is firm, the top is rounded and golden-brown, and a knife inserted in the centre comes out clean — 45–50 minutes.
9. Let the quiche sit for 5 minutes before slicing (it will sink slightly as it cools).

### Enjoy!
