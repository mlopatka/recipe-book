# Cucumber Sunomono
## Cucumber Salad (酢の物)
_Japanese_, _salad_, _vegetarian_, _side_  
Preparation time: 15 mins  
4 small portions  

## Ingredients
* 3 Japanese cucumbers, or 4 Persian cucumbers
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
* 1 tablespoon sugar
* 1/4 teaspoon soy sauce
* 1 teaspoon sesame seeds

## Preparation
1. Slice cucumbers as thinly as possible. Sprinkle with salt and let sit for 5 minutes. Squeeze out as much water as possible from the cucumbers.
2. In a small bowl, mix rice vinegar, sugar and soy sauce together until sugar dissolves.
3. Add the vinegar mixture and sesame seeds to the prepared cucumbers and mix well.
4. Serve immediately or refrigerate briefly before serving.
