# Glühwein
## Dutch/German Mulled Wine
_Dutch_, _German_, _drinks_, _winter_, _festive_  
Preparation time: ⚠️ TODO — not recorded in source  
Makes ~8–10 glasses  

## Ingredients
* 1.5 L red wine
* 50 cl (500 ml) brandy
* 50 cl (500 ml) water
* 2 oranges
* 2 sticks cinnamon (pijpkaneel)
* 2 star anise
* 12 cloves (kruidnagels)
* 4 tablespoons sugar

## Preparation
⚠️ **Instructions were not included in the source file.** Standard Glühwein method:

* Peel the zest from the oranges in wide strips, avoiding the white pith. Squeeze the juice from the oranges.
* Combine all ingredients in a large pot over medium heat. Stir to dissolve the sugar.
* Heat gently — do not boil — until steaming and fragrant, about 20–30 minutes.
* Taste and adjust sweetness or spicing.
* Strain into mugs and serve hot, optionally garnished with a cinnamon stick or orange slice.
